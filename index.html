Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Timeline of MS Dhoni's career:

  • 1981 Dhoni was born on July 7, 1981 in Ranchi, Bihar (now Jharkhand) to Pan Singh and Devaki Devi..
  • 1998 M. S. Dhoni, who had been playing only in school and club level cricket till then, was selected to play for the Central Coal Fields Limited (CCL) team.
  • 2004 He made his ODI debut against Bangladesh.
  • 2007 Dhoni took over the captaincy of the one-day side and also led India to the Twenty20 (T20) world title.
  • 2007-08 M.S. Dhoni has been awarded the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna award, for his contributions and achievements in sports.
  • 2008 For his performances in 2008, he was named as wicket keeper of the World ODI XI by the ICC.
  • 2011 Dhoni’s dashing innings of 91 not out—in front of a home crowd in Mumbai—paved the way for India’s victory over Sri Lanka in the ODI world cup final.
  • 2011 M S Dhoni was the CNN-IBN Indian of the Year in Sports.
  • 2013 MS Dhoni became the only Indian captain who has led India to all three major ICC events by winning Champion Trophy in 2013.
  • 2014 MS Dhoni retired from test Cricket in mid of a series.
  • 2015 Forbes ranked Dhoni at 23rd in the list of highest paid athletes in the world.
  • 2017 Dhoni quit the ODI and T20I captaincy to hand over the reign to Kohli.
  • 2018 MS Dhoni received Padma Bhushan Award.
